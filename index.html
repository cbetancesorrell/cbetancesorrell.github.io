LGBT Network Winter Ball
Commemorating World AIDS day

A majestic Winter Ball was hosted by the LGBT Network of Long Island on Friday, December 1st in commemoration of World AIDS Day. The annual event, which was held at Crest Hollow Country Club in Woodbury, preceded its silver anniversary this year. Over $10,000 were raised in funds, and the Nework will use the proceeds to support HIV/AIDS testing, outreach, prevention, education, and support services.

There is estimated over 1 million people within the United States that are living with HIV today, yet 1 in 7 might not know that they have it. But from 2008 to now, the annual number of HIV infections has gone down over eighteen percent. This is partly due to organizations like the LGBT Network who put in continous hours of effort to improve the outreach, education and support services that make all the difference.

Diana Santariello
LGBT Network Winter Ball 2017 Honoree
